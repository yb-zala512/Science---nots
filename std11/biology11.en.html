1) Which of the following is a main category in biological classification?

A) Species

B) Kingdom

C) Division

D) Key

✅ Answer: D) Key

2) What is the estimated total number of known living species in the world?

A) 1.25 million species

B) 8.5 million species

C) 1.025 million species

D) 1.7 million species

✅ Answer: B) 8.5 million species

3) A family in taxonomy mainly means...

A) Group of related families

B) Group of related genera

C) A random group of any ranking

D) The number and type of organisms present on Earth

✅ Answer: B) Group of related genera

4) Scientific names should always use which language?

A) Latin

B) Hindi

C) Greek

D) Sanskrit

✅ Answer: A) Latin

5) Choose the correct explanation for the statement:
A: Literature about scientific names should be in Latin.
R: Latin has beauty for naming.

A) A and R are both correct and R is correct explanation of A

B) A and R are both correct but R is not correct explanation of A

C) A is correct and R is false

D) A is false and R is true

✅ Answer: B) A and R are both correct but R is not correct explanation of A

6) Benefit of classification system:

A) It saves effort and reduces description work

B) It gives importance to animals and plants

C) All categories are like a phylum

D) Useful only for smaller categories

✅ Answer: A) It saves effort and reduces description work

7) Choose the correct option:
A: Anatomy and external features are usually similar.
R: Organs may look similar but work differently.

A) Both A and R are true, and R explains A

B) Both A and R are true, but R does not explain A

C) A is true and R is false

D) A is false and R is true

✅ Answer: B) Both A and R are true, but R does not explain A
